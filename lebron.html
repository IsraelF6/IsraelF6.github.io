The Man



  • The Player
  • The Worker
  • The Philanthropist
  • The Activist
  • The Father


During his 15 year NBA career, He has won 3 championships with 3 NBA finals MVP awards.



4 regular season MVP awards, 15 time NBA All-Star, 6 time NBA All-Defense, along with countless other records and accolades



Despite his success, he continues to develop his game by increasing his 3-pt shooting percentage and taking more efficient shots.



From personal chef's to massages and Hyperbaric Chambers, he reportedly spends $1.5 million per year to take care of his body



Along with the Lebron James Family Foundation, he has pledged to provide students in his hometown of Akron, Ohio with full scholarships, estimated at more than $41 Million.



Signed a seven-year, $93 million deal to Nike right out of high school, and in December 2015 signed a lifetime deal reportedly worth over $1 Billion.



Ownership in Liverpool FC, Beats headphones, Sprite, Kia, and Blaze Pizza restaurants; the list of investments and endorsements seem endless.



LeBron has spoken out and even taken the lead in many civil rights issues despite the backlash. Seen here sporting a rare version of his shoes.



At the ESPN award shows, ESPY's, with a few friends (Dwyane Wade, Chris Paul, & Carmelo Anthony) speaking out against gun violence.



LeBron during warm ups before a game showing condolences to Eric Gradner who died following a conforntation with a NYPD officer. "I can't breathe" were his last words.



LeBron, his wife, and his sons celebrating on court after advancing to the NBA Finals.



LeBron celebrating with his 2 sons Bryce and Bron Jr. after a win.



He married his high school sweetheart, Savannah, and has been with her ever since.
